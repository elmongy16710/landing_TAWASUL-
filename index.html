ABOUT TAWASUL

Tawasul, is a toll-free, country-wide helpline number, established to enable affected populations to easily access information related to humanitarian assistance and establish a two-way communication between the affected population and the humanitarian community to provide feedback and improve accountability.

MEDICAL ASSISTANCE

Provide Information on available health and medical programs, as well as assistance in guiding beneficiaries to the appropriate facility to receive the required assistance, also, Tawasul immediately refers Emergency and reproductive health related cases to the designated agency

PROTECTION, CHILD PROTECTION & GBV

We offer counselling to those in need while maintaining their privacy, and we provide valuable information about security programs and protection services. When an individual is at risk of significant harm, Tawasul immediately reports the case to the concerned agency to provide protection.
